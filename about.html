About Me

Leadership & Impact

I believe in using technology to solve real-world problems. As the President of my school's INTERalliance chapter, I grew club membership by 166% (from 15 to over 40 members) and launched the EagleVision project to create affordable, digitized microscopes for low-income schools. As the Programming Lead for FTC Robotics Team 6133, I develop computer vision solutions using neural networks and OpenCV and create documentation to help new members learn GitHub and Android Studio.

Teaching & Community

I'm passionate about sharing knowledge. I co-founded and serve as an assistant teacher for the first online Arduino course in Kurdistan, mentoring students in robotics and electronics. I also teach online Arabic pronunciation classes to youth and served as the Head of Communication for the Islamic Association of Cincinnati's Ramadan Iftar events.
