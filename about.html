About Me

The Intersection of Biology & Technology

I am an aspiring Biomedical Engineer and developer passionate about the convergence of hardware, software, and human biology. My work focuses on Biomechatronics—creating systems where digital logic meets physical motion. Whether I am designing 3D-printed robotics, architecting low-latency video platforms for education, or developing computer vision algorithms, my goal remains the same: to use engineering to extend human capability and solve accessibility problems.

Community & Education

I believe that innovation creates the most value when it is shared. I am deeply committed to democratizing education, both locally and globally. My background involves mentoring students in robotics, developing curriculum for the first online Arduino course in Kurdistan, and serving my local Muslim community through youth leadership. I strive to build technology that bridges gaps—whether those gaps are economic, educational, or physical.
