Limelight Vision Orientation Feb 2025

Limelight 3A: Advanced Vision

Advanced our vision system to detect game piece orientation through aspect ratio analysis, enabling precise autonomous control and handling of multiple colored samples.

Building on our initial Python SnapScript work, we achieved a major breakthrough in our Leviathan robot's vision system.
